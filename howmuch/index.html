How Much

A Currency Converter

How Much is a simple to use currency converter that I basically made for myself to use while traveling.
Hopefully it is useful for you too.
Foreign Exchange information provided by Alpha Vantage.
I have no relation to Alpha Vantage, I just use their API to download the currency rate.
